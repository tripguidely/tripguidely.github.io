How to choose the right Universal Studios ticket

Standard tickets

Best if you want the most straightforward entry option and already know which park fits your destination.

Express or faster-access options

Worth considering on busier dates or when your schedule is tight and you want to reduce time lost in lines.

Peak date planning

Book early if you are traveling around weekends, holidays or high-demand seasons. Waiting too long can limit your options.
