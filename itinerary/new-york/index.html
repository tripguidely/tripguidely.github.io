The TripGuidely method (why these plans work)

A simple framework you can reuse without wrecking the trip.

  • Anchor first: pick the timed ticket that shapes the half-day (Statue ferry, 9/11 Museum, SUMMIT, Top of the Rock).
  • Neighborhood clusters: Downtown + Brooklyn together, Midtown + Uptown together.
  • Buffers: subway delays, security checks, and elevator queues add friction. We plan for it.
  • Optional layers: helicopter, cruise, or Broadway only after anchors are locked.
