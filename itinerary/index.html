FAQ

Quick answers for itinerary planning.

How many days do I need in Paris?

For most travelers, 3 days is the best balance (big sights + neighborhood time). If you want a day trip (Versailles/Normandy), plan 4+ days.

Do I need a car for city itineraries?

Inside major cities, you usually don’t. A car is most useful for day trips and countryside routes—compare options and pickup locations before booking.

What should I book first?

Prioritize timed-entry tickets (major museums/landmarks), then hotels, then tours. eSIM can be done anytime but is easiest before you fly.
