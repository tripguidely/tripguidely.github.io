FAQ

Quick answers aligned with itinerary search intent.

What’s the best itinerary length for a first trip?

3 days is the most balanced for most cities: enough time for major sights plus neighborhoods. Choose 2 days for highlights only, and 4 days if you want a day trip.

What should I book first to avoid long lines?

Start with timed-entry tickets for major attractions, then your hotel, then tours. Tickets set your daily time blocks and prevent itinerary breakage.

Do I need a car for a city itinerary?

Usually not inside the city. A car is most useful for day trips and countryside routes. Use the car rental hub if you plan day trips.

How do I make this itinerary “conversion ready”?

After value: add contextual links inside each day (tickets/tours), then “Where to stay” (hotels), then “Stay connected” (eSIM), then “Day trips” (car rental). Don’t interrupt the day-by-day flow.
