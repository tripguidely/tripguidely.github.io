Transport hub FAQ

Should I use trains or rail passes?

Use trains when you already know the route you want. Use rail passes when you expect multiple train journeys or want broader rail flexibility across cities or regions.

When is car rental the better choice?

Car rental often makes more sense when your trip includes rural areas, multiple stops, airport pick-up, or destinations with weaker public transport coverage.

When should I use ferries or cruises?

Use ferries for direct island or port-to-port transport. Use cruises when the goal is itinerary-based travel across a broader destination region.
