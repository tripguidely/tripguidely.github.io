Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-06

This Privacy Policy explains how TripGuidely (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit tripguidely.github.io (the “Site”).

Important: We aim to collect as little personal information as reasonably possible while using analytics, affiliate tools, and basic technical services to operate and improve the Site.

1) Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Site, we may collect or receive limited categories of information, including:

  • Usage data: pages visited, approximate region, device/browser type, referral source, and general engagement signals.
  • Interaction data: actions such as outbound link clicks and navigation behavior used to understand which content and resources are useful.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser information, and similar technical identifiers that may be processed for analytics, security, fraud prevention, or site operations.

2) Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to better understand how visitors use the Site and to improve content, navigation, and user experience.

Analytics data may include pages viewed, engagement signals, approximate region, device type, browser type, and traffic source information. We configure analytics settings to reduce identifiability where reasonably possible, including disabling Google signals and ad personalization signals.

3) Outbound link tracking

To understand which resources are most useful, we may measure outbound link clicks, meaning clicks to websites outside TripGuidely.

This measurement may include the destination URL and related event metadata for reporting, content improvement, and performance analysis.

4) Affiliate links

Some links on the Site may be affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, booking, or reservation, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Affiliate links help support the Site and its editorial work. For more details, please review our Affiliate Disclosure.

5) Third-party scripts and partners

The Site may use third-party scripts, tags, widgets, or tools that support analytics, affiliate attribution, verification, monetization, or embedded functionality.

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and practices. Examples may include:

  • Google (Analytics / Google tag)
  • Travelpayouts and related affiliate tools
  • Other travel, booking, attribution, or monetization partners used across the Site

6) Cookies and similar technologies

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies from analytics providers, affiliate partners, and technical service providers.

These technologies may be used to:

  • measure site performance and visitor behavior,
  • support affiliate attribution,
  • improve content and navigation,
  • help with security, fraud prevention, and basic functionality.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality, attribution, analytics accuracy, or the performance of some third-party tools.

7) Your choices

  • Browser controls: You can block, limit, or delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • Analytics and privacy settings: You may also use browser tools, extensions, or device settings that reduce analytics tracking or cookie storage.
  • Privacy requests: If you have questions about your data or this policy, you can contact us directly using the details below.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers send DNT signals. Because there is no universal standard for DNT handling, the Site may not respond consistently to those signals.

8) Data retention

We retain analytics and related reporting data only for as long as reasonably necessary for performance analysis, trend reporting, site improvement, security review, and operational purposes.

Retention periods may also depend on the settings and practices of third-party platforms we use.

9) Children’s privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10) Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the Site and its data. However, no method of transmission, storage, or online processing is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11) International visitors

TripGuidely is available to readers in multiple regions. Depending on where you access the Site from, your information may be processed by service providers operating in different jurisdictions.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent version.

13) Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or privacy-related concerns, you can contact us through our Contact page or by email:

  • Email: tripguidely@hotmail.com

You can also learn more about TripGuidely on our About page and review our Affiliate Disclosure.
