How to rent smart

How to rent a car without overpaying

  • Compare first: prices vary wildly by location and timing.
  • Understand insurance: full coverage often costs less online than at the counter.
  • Check deposits: know how much will be blocked on your card.
  • Inspect the car: photos at pickup save headaches later.
Tip: Airport rentals are convenient, city rentals are often cheaper.

When you actually need a car

Cars make sense for road trips, countryside travel, or places with limited public transport. In dense cities, combine walking + transit and rent only for day trips.
