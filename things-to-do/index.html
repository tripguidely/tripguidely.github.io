How to choose the right activity

  • Time window: match activity length to your day (and transit time).
  • Weather backup: keep a “rain plan” (museum/show) ready.
  • Peak hours: book early for sunrise/sunset, weekends, and holidays.
  • Location cluster: choose activities close together to avoid wasted time.

FAQ

Should this page force a city?

Not ideally. A hub page should stay neutral so the user’s destination drives the results. Use city pages as the main booking entry points.

USD or CAD for currency?

USD is a global default; CAD can reduce friction for Canada-first traffic if the tool supports it reliably.

What should I track with SUB ID?

Keep one ID for this hub (ex: things-hub). Later, add different sub-IDs per placement (hero, mid-page) for cleaner attribution.
