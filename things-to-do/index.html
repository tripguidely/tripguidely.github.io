How to choose the right activity

  • Time window: match duration + transit so your day stays smooth.
  • Peak hours: book early slots for the big attractions (weekends, holidays).
  • Weather backup: keep one indoor option as your plan B.
  • Location cluster: stack activities that are close to avoid zig-zagging.
What should I track with SubID?

Keep one hub ID (ex: things-hub). When you add more widgets later, use different sub-IDs for placements (hero, mid-page, footer) for clean attribution.

FAQ

USD or CAD for currency?

USD is a global default. If most of your traffic is Canada-first and the widget supports CAD reliably, CAD can reduce friction.

Should this hub force a destination?

No. A hub should stay neutral. The city pages are where you go hard on “best-of” lists, passes, and itineraries.
