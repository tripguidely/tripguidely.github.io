How to choose the right activity

Simple rules that prevent itinerary chaos.

  • Time window: match duration + transit so your day stays smooth.
  • Peak hours: book early slots for big attractions (weekends, holidays).
  • Weather backup: keep one indoor option as your plan B.
  • Location cluster: stack nearby activities to avoid zig-zagging.
  • Energy curve: do “standing + walking” early, save seated experiences later.

FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

USD or CAD for currency?

USD is a global default. If most of your traffic is Canada-first and the widget supports CAD reliably, CAD can reduce friction.

Should this hub force a destination?

No. A hub should stay neutral. The destination pages are where you publish best-of lists, passes, and itineraries.

How many activities should I book in advance?

Book 1–2 “anchor” items first (top attraction or timed entry), then keep 1–2 flexible options. This prevents your day from collapsing if transit or weather changes.
