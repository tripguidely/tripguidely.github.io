Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: 2026-03-06

TripGuidely participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that some links on this website may be affiliate links.

Important: If you click an affiliate link and make a booking or purchase, TripGuidely may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

How affiliate links work

If you click on an affiliate link and complete a booking, reservation, or purchase, we may receive a small commission from the partner platform or service provider. This does not increase the price you pay.

Not every link on TripGuidely is an affiliate link. Some links are included for informational, editorial, or navigation purposes only.

Why TripGuidely uses affiliate links

Affiliate commissions help support the operation of this website, including research, writing, editing, technical maintenance, and ongoing updates to our travel content.

Our goal is to publish structured, practical travel information that helps readers compare options, plan more efficiently, and make informed booking decisions. Affiliate partnerships help us continue offering that content for free.

Editorial independence

Our recommendations are based on independent research, practical planning value, destination relevance, usability, and clear decision-making criteria.

Compensation does not determine whether a destination, service, or booking option is mentioned. We aim to present useful information, clear trade-offs, and relevant planning considerations based on traveler intent.

Where affiliate links may appear

Affiliate links may appear across TripGuidely, including but not limited to:

  • Destination guides
  • Hotel and accommodation pages
  • Things-to-do and attraction pages
  • Cruise content
  • Car rental pages
  • eSIM and travel connectivity content
  • Travel planning tools and booking recommendations

Affiliate partners and categories

TripGuidely may participate in travel affiliate programs involving categories such as:

  • Flights and travel booking platforms
  • Hotels and accommodation providers
  • Tours, attractions, and activity platforms
  • Cruise booking partners
  • Car rental platforms
  • Travel technology and connectivity services

Specific affiliate partners may change over time as we refine booking options, improve the reader experience, and update monetization partners.

Your trust matters
